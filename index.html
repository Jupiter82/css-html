Welcome to College of Information Technology & Engineering

College of Information Technology and Engineering (CITE) was established to address the acute shortage of IT, Engineering and Management professionals in Nepal and abroad. The College is guided by values that place the students at the centre of the learning experience and inculcate high professional standards of quality and discipline among them. At College of Information Technology and Engineering (CITE), students are challenged to acquire new skills. They study emerging theory and learn about new discoveries and technologies.
